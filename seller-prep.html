Shoot-day prep · about 10 minutes

Your home's camera day is coming up.

Great listing photos and video sell homes faster — and 90% of "great" happens before we even ring the doorbell. Here's the short list. Tap items as you go.

⏱ 10 minutes the night before · 5 the morning of
01The night before
✓Clear the countersKitchen and bathrooms — appliances, dish racks, soaps, toothbrushes into a drawer. Bare counters read "spacious" on camera.
✓Declutter what the camera seesShoes by the door, cords, laundry, fridge magnets, garbage bins. A laundry basket makes a great 5-minute hiding spot.
✓Make every bed like a hotelSmooth duvets, fluffed pillows. Beds are the first thing the camera lands on in a bedroom.
✓Tuck away personal photos & valuablesFor your privacy and security — family photos, documents, jewellery, medications out of sight.
✓Replace any burnt-out bulbsEvery light gets turned on for the shoot — one dead bulb is surprisingly visible. Matching warm-white looks best.
✓Tidy the yard & entranceHose off the porch, hide the bins and hoses, straighten cushions, mow if it's shaggy. The front of the house is photo #1.
02The morning of
✓Turn on every light, open every blindLamps too. Bright rooms look bigger, warmer and more expensive. We'll fine-tune from there.
✓Hide the daily-life extrasPet bowls, litter boxes, kids' toys, remotes, chargers, paper piles — one quick sweep.
✓Clear the drivewayMove cars off the driveway and away from the front of the house — for exterior shots and drone, if booked.
✓Set the scene, lightlyA bowl of fruit, fresh towels folded in bathrooms, toilet lids down, chairs tucked in. Small touches, big difference.
✓Plan for petsWe love them — the camera doesn't. A walk, a crate, or a neighbour visit during the shoot works perfectly.
03While we shoot
✓Give us the run of the houseShoots take roughly 45–90 minutes. It's easiest (and fastest) if rooms stay empty while we work through them.
✓No need to follow us aroundWe've photographed hundreds of homes — we'll treat yours with care, close what we open, and leave everything as we found it.
Why this matters: listings with professional photos and video get more views, more showings, and routinely sell faster. The ten minutes you spend on this list shows up in every single frame — and in the offers.
